Tuning: Standard

I play this with my fingers, not a pick. Hit the low notes with your thumb, 
and pluck the higher notes with your index and middle fingers. It might
also make it easier if you grab the low notes with your thumb on your
fretting hand. The pattern is pretty much the same throughout the whole 
song. Full lyrics are at the bottom.
