At the end of the last measure, a drop pedal is used right on the return to the Gm chord. 
I use a DigiTech whammy pedal with the "Down 2 Octaves" and "Chord" settings. After the
pedal is hit the chord also changes to G Major. Let off on the pedal as the tempo picks up
until you are fully back to normal playing G (355433) the whole time).
